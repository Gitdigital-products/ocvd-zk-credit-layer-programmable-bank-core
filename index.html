How It Works

1

Generate Proof

Your client generates a ZK proof using your private credit score and secret key.

2

Submit Public Signals

Only the threshold (750), nullifier hash, and your address are sent on-chain.

3

Verify & Unlock

The contract verifies the proof and unlocks 120% LTV borrowing power.

Circuit: credit_score.circom Compiled
pragma circom 2.0.0;
template CreditScore() {
signal input creditScore;
signal input threshold;
signal output isValid;
isValid <== creditScore >= threshold;
}
